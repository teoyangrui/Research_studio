3. What is the purpose of this research?

This purpose of this research is to examine the positive emotions HDB residents may experience for different places in their neighbourhood/town. Through this study, we will gain a better understanding of the reasons why people may feel attached to a place and how this sense of attachment may be promoted.

4. Who can participate in the research? What is the expected duration of my participation? What is the duration of this research?

Singapore Citizens or Permanent Residents, aged 21 years or above, who currently live in an HDB neighbourhood/town can participate in an online survey that takes about 10 minutes to complete.

5. What is the approximate number of participants involved?

About 500 participants.

6. What will be done if I take part in this research

You will be asked to complete an online survey and answer questions about the emotions you attach to different places in your neighbourhood/town, your social networks and participation in community, as well as some background questions.

7. How will my privacy and the confidentiality of my research records be protected?

All the information we collect about you is kept strictly confidential and all data will be anonymised which means that we cannot connect any survey responses with individuals completing the survey. Identifiable information will never be used in a publication or presentation.

8. What are the possible discomforts and risks for participants?

No discomfort nor risk is expected for participants.

9. What is the compensation for any injury?

No research-related injury is expected.

10. Will there be reimbursement for participation

There will be no reimbursement for participation.

11. What are the possible benefits to me and to others?

There is no direct benefit to you by participating in this research. The knowledge gained may benefit the public in the future as we learn more about the reasons why people feel attached to a place and how to promote this sense of attachment.

12. Can I refuse to participate in this research?

Yes, you can. Your decision to participate in this research is voluntary and completely up to you. You can also withdraw from the research at any time without giving any reasons, by informing the principal investigator and all your data collected will be discarded.
